## Free Prompts

Try these prompts with any AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini).

### Proposals & Quotes

**1. Business Idea Evaluation and Scoring**
> Act as a Business Idea Evaluator. You will analyze a business idea and provide a comprehensive evaluation across multiple dimensions including market potential, feasibility, competitive landscape, and financial viability. Score each dimension and provide actionable recommendations.

**2. Pitch**
> Write a compelling elevator pitch for your product or service. Focus on the problem you solve, your target audience, and what makes you different.

**3. Startup Idea Generator**
> Generate innovative startup ideas based on market trends, emerging technologies, and consumer needs. For each idea, provide the problem statement, target market, revenue model, and competitive advantage.

**4. Business Strategy Consultant**
> Analyze a business situation and provide strategic recommendations. Consider market position, competitive threats, growth opportunities, and operational efficiency.

**5. Case Study Writer**
> Write a professional case study that showcases how a product or service solved a specific problem for a client. Include background, challenge, solution, and results.

### Client Emails

**1. Email Marketing Specialist**
> Act as an email marketing specialist. Write an engaging email sequence for your target audience. Include subject line, preview text, body copy, and call-to-action.

**2. Cover Letter Writer**
> Write a professional cover letter tailored to a specific job description. Highlight relevant experience, skills, and achievements that match the requirements.

**3. Follow-up Email**
> Write a polite follow-up email after a meeting or interview. Express continued interest and reiterate key talking points.

**4. Newsletter Writer**
> Write an engaging newsletter for your subscribers. Include a compelling subject line, valuable content, and a clear call-to-action.

**5. Cold Outreach**
> Write a personalized cold outreach email. Research the recipient's background, identify mutual connections or interests, and propose a clear value exchange.

### Social Media & Content

**1. Social Media Manager**
> Create a social media content calendar for the week. Include platform-specific posts, optimal posting times, hashtag strategies, and engagement tactics.

**2. Copywriting Expert**
> Write persuasive copy for a landing page. Include a compelling headline, benefit-driven subheadings, social proof, and a strong call-to-action.

**3. Content Creator**
> Turn a complex topic into engaging social media content. Break down the key points, create hooks, and format for different platforms.

**4. SEO Content Writer**
> Write SEO-optimized blog content around a target keyword. Include meta description, headings, body content with keyword placement, and internal linking suggestions.

**5. Brand Strategist**
> Develop a brand voice and messaging framework. Define tone, key messages, value proposition, and brand personality traits.
